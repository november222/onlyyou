Privacy Policy

Last updated:

OnlyYou collects and processes personal data solely to provide core app features such as authentication, messaging, and premium subscriptions. We do not sell your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, name, avatar.
  • App usage data: connection sessions, preferences.
  • Purchase data: subscription status and receipt metadata (no full payment info is stored by us).

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and secure access.
  • Provide features such as connection and messaging.
  • Verify and manage premium subscriptions.
  • Improve reliability and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We may use service providers (e.g., Supabase) to host and process data on our behalf under strict security and confidentiality obligations.

Security

We apply authentication, access control, and encryption in transit. No method is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our safeguards.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at support@onlyyou.app.

Contact
